The math behind the pilot

Most software orgs leave six or seven figures of credit on the table — before state.

At a fully-loaded $180K/engineer and 55% qualifying R&D time, a typical product-shipping software org sees a conservative 8% federal ASC credit on QRE. California adds ~7.5% effective on top; Massachusetts ~5%; New York ~6%.

Most of that gets claimed. What doesn't get claimed is the audit defensibility — which is why preparers haircut aggressive claims 20–40%. Tymr's pilot removes the haircut by putting a source event behind every hour.
