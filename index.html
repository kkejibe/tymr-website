How it works

Three steps. No timesheets at any of them.

You don't interrupt your engineers. You don't re-platform your team. Tymr reads the tools your team already uses — and does the rest continuously.

01 · Collect

Connect Jira, GitHub, and your calendar.

OAuth setup in under an hour. Tymr reads event streams — ticket transitions, commits, meeting blocks — in real time. No desktop agent, no browser plugin, no engineer survey.

Event-level ingest · not summaries
02 · Classify

Every hour gets tagged automatically.

Each Jira transition, commit, and calendar block is attributed to the engineer and project, then classified: R&D-qualified, software CapEx, or OpEx. Low-confidence calls go to a human for a 30-minute weekly review.

Auto-tagged · reviewable · cited to source
03 · Deliver

Four files, ready for your tax firm.

At quarter-end, Tymr produces a credit estimate, a CapEx / OpEx pack, a workpaper-ready CSV, and a 2-page methodology PDF. Your tax firm drops them into the same workpapers they use today.

PDF + CSV · preparer-ready
