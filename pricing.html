How does the R&D credit payback math work?

A 100-engineer software company with a fully-loaded dev cost of $180K/engineer spends roughly $18M/year on engineering. If 55% of that work qualifies for the US federal R&D tax credit (typical for companies shipping new product), that's ~$9.9M in Qualified Research Expenses — an estimated $1.4M federal credit under the ASC method (plus state credits).

Tymr's Growth tier for that org costs ~$14K/year. You don't need the credit to grow by much for Tymr to pay for itself 50× over — and that's before counting the auditor hours you save on SOX capitalization.
