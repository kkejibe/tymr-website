Common questions

Do you have a free trial on paid tiers?

Email us — we'll set up a sandbox so you can see the live capacity ledger (and the compliance pack underneath) against representative data before you decide.

How do you bill?

Monthly or annual, your choice. Annual saves about 15%.

What counts as a seat?

Any human who logs into Tymr. AI agents are tracked separately and don't count as seats.

Do I have to use every feature in a tier to pay for it?

No. Use what's useful. The tier just unlocks what's available.
